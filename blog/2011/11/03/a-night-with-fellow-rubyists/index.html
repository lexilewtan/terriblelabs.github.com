A Night With Fellow Rubyists and Railsists

Posted by Terrible Labs, Inc. Nov 3rd, 2011 | Comments

We’re excited to be sponsoring the first Bubbly Bub & Grub at a Pub in the Hub Club.  If you’re looking to grab some beers and food while catching up with fellow Rubyists, definitely come and hang.  Just make sure to snag a free ticket.

We will all be gathering at The Black Rose in Quincy Market on November 29th from 6pm to 9pm.

A big thanks to Brian Cardarella for making this event happen!
